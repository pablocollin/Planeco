About us

Planeco was born on a snowy Friday night in a small university residence room in Montreal. While all our friends were out partying, we stayed in and brainstormed to figure out what we were going to design for the 11th codeJam hackathon. After hours of tossing and turning, we came up with the idea that was going to change the way companies think their schedule building: Planeco. Indeed, it addresses the 2 biggest challenges that companies face during the 21st century: sustainability, to limit their environmental impact, and remote work, to protect their workers from pandemics but also to limit costs by optimizing the size of their in-presence workspaces.

How it works

Our algorithm takes schedule building to a whole new level by including the carbon footprint of each employee when they make their way to work, prioritizing the ones using the most eco-friendly means of transport. We also consider the employee’s comfort by including each employee’s preferred days for remote work and the time it takes them to reach the company’s workspace when we build the weekly schedule. Finally, our algorithm ensures itself that the maximum capacity of a workspace is never exceeded, automatically adapting it to potential sanitary restrictions.
